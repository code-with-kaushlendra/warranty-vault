Warranty Claims

To file a claim, go to the warranty details page and click "File Claim". Follow the guided process to provide details about the issue and upload supporting documents. Our team will review and guide you through the next steps.

Most claims are processed within 5-7 business days. Complex claims may take longer. You'll receive updates via email throughout the process.

If your claim is denied, we'll provide a detailed explanation. You can appeal the decision or contact our support team for assistance.
