Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload my warranties?

+

You can simply take a photo of your bill, upload a PDF, or scan a QR code if provided by the retailer. Our system makes it incredibly easy to add warranties in just a few seconds.

Is my data safe?

+

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption and do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your warranty information is protected with the same security standards used by banks.

Can I manage warranties for my whole family?

+

Yes, with our Concierge Plus plan, you can create one master account for your entire family. You can manage up to 5 family members' warranties from a single dashboard.

What happens when my warranty is about to expire?

+

We send you smart reminders via your preferred channel (WhatsApp, SMS, or in-app notification) at 30, 15, and 7 days before expiry. You'll never miss an important warranty deadline.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

+

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time without any hidden charges or penalties. Your data will remain accessible for 30 days after cancellation.

Do you offer customer support?

+

Yes! We offer email support for all plans, dedicated chatbot support for Smart Guardian and above, and personal concierge assistance for Concierge Plus members.
